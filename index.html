About Me

I'm currently a Junior Undergrad student at UIC, majoring in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering.

My coding journey began in High School at Taft High School in Chicago, where I graduated in 2021. It all started with a computer science class during my sophomore year, and I was immediately drawn to coding. During my senior year, amidst the challenges of COVID-19, I took an online Robotics class where we delved into Arduino programming. Despite the virtual setup, my teacher's dedication stood out as he even visited my home to provide me with an Arduino kit. That experience sparked my fascination with Arduino and solidified my decision to pursue a Computer Science major in college.

Since then, I've taken various CS classes such as Program Design I and II, Foundations of Computing, Programming Practicum, Data Structures, Machine Organization, Software Design, Languages and Automata, Systems Programming, and Computer Design. Each class has contributed significantly to my growth and passion for the field.
